Earth's Mightiest Heroes

Join forces with the Avengers and discover your strength.

Iron Man

Tech genius Tony Stark leads with intellect and innovation.

  • Genius-level intellect
  • Powered by the Arc Reactor
  • Skilled engineer and inventor
  • Advanced combat skills with his suit

Captain America

Steve Rogers stands for justice, courage, and valor.

  • Super Soldier Serum enhanced strength
  • Master tactician and hand-to-hand combatant
  • Indestructible shield made of Vibranium
  • Unwavering moral compass

Thor

The God of Thunder, protector of realms, and wielder of Mjölnir.

  • Wields Mjölnir and Stormbreaker
  • Immortal Asgardian with god-like strength
  • Master of lightning and storms
  • Defender of Asgard and Midgard

Black Widow

Natalia Romanova is a master spy and skilled in combat.

  • Expert in espionage and infiltration
  • Highly trained in hand-to-hand combat
  • Master marksman and skilled with various weapons
  • Formidable strategist and tactician

Spider-Man

Peter Parker swings into action with agility, web-slinging, and a witty charm.

  • Enhanced agility and reflexes
  • Spider-sense to detect danger
  • Web-slinging ability with custom web-shooters
  • Intelligent and resourceful

Hulk

Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk, embodying unstoppable strength.

  • Superhuman strength, durability, and regeneration
  • Ability to grow stronger with rage
  • Highly resistant to physical damage
  • Unpredictable and volatile, but loyal

Dr. Strange

Master of the mystic arts, Stephen Strange protects reality with his magical powers.

  • Master of sorcery and the mystic arts
  • Wields the Eye of Agamotto
  • Expert in dimensional magic and time manipulation
  • Guardian of the Earth’s magical defenses

Black Panther

King T'Challa of Wakanda fights with honor and the power of the Black Panther.

  • Enhanced strength, speed, and agility
  • Master hand-to-hand combatant and strategist
  • Wears the Vibranium suit for enhanced durability
  • Ruler of the technologically advanced nation of Wakanda
